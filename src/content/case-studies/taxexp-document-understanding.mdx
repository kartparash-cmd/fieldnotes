## The customer scenario

CPA firm or enterprise tax/finance team automating high-volume structured document generation needs reliable extraction, validation, and human-in-the-loop fallback.

## My product canvas

This case study is grounded in **TaxEXP** — an automated K1 and Form 1065 tax document generation pipeline. CPA variant adapts the same engine for accounting practice workflows. Canonical writeup: [kaydenlabs.com/work](https://kaydenlabs.com/work).

## Architectural patterns to be demonstrated

- **High-volume structured document pipeline** — K1, Form 1065, with field-level extraction and validation
- **Confidence-scored output with HITL fallback at threshold** — model owns the high-confidence rows; humans own the low-confidence
- **Cost modeling across Haiku / Sonnet / Opus** — production routing by document complexity and field criticality
