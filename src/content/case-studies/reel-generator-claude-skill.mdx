## The customer scenario

Marketing or content operations team needs a custom Claude Skill that automates a domain-specific creative workflow with evaluation and quality gates.

## My product canvas

This case study is grounded in **Reel Generator** — an automated viral TikTok pipeline packaged as a custom Claude Skill. Brand voice, format constraints, and quality gates baked into the agentic workflow. Canonical writeup: [kaydenlabs.com/work](https://kaydenlabs.com/work).

## Architectural patterns to be demonstrated

- **Custom Claude Skill packaging** — domain-specific creative workflow as a portable, reusable skill
- **Quality gates in an agentic publishing pipeline** — script generation → review → voiceover → caption → publish, each gated
- **Brand-voice eval methodology** — paired-comparison evals against a reference corpus
